OREO
Breed: Siamese Cat
Height: 25 cm
Weight: 7 kgs
Age: 6 years
Gender: Male
Location: Kolhapur, Maharashtra, India
Highlights

1) They are bestowed with big paws, triangular ears, long legs and blue eyes. They are different from other types of cats as they have spots of different colours like chocolate, black and blue.
2) They are curious, loyal, caring, playful and intelligent and calm.


Guide

1) They are attached to their humans like a baby.
2) Scratching posts and plenty of cat toys are essential, with puzzle toys a good choice for keeping them busy.
3) They can be destructive if given too much time alone.
