HICCUP
Breed: Boxer
Height: 22.5 inches
Weight: 60 kgs
Age: 6 years
Gender: Female
Location: Pune, Maharashtra, India

Highlights

1) It is a very intelligent, sporty dog which is very easy to train.
2) Also, it is one of the best guard dogs to keep. It has a large body and head, a square muzzle and strong jaws.
3) It is gentle, friendly, quiet and protective earned it a reputation as a great dog with children.


Guide

1) The Boxer`s short, shiny coat requires very little grooming. Its nails should be trimmed regularly.
2) Early socialization and puppy training classes are necessary for this breed.
3) They are highly intelligent and tend to have a mind of their own and are excellent problem solvers.
