SPLASH
Breed: Lion-head Rabbit
Height: 9 inches
Weight: 1.5 kgs
Age: 2.5 years
Gender: Female
Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
Highlights

1) Lionheads are extremely good natured, and combined with the genetic mutation that gives them the long fur around their heads are not only a pleasant pet to spend time with, but pleasant to look at as well.
2) They are happy to cuddle up and enjoy as much attention as they can get.


Guide

1) As pets, these rabbits are generally friendly, playful, and social, though some can be timid.
2) They require daily interaction and a moderate amount of maintenance.
